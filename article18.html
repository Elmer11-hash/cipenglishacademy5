"Level System"

The following is a table of our levels and conversion scores to official scores.
To choose the IELTS Intensive course, the student's admission level must be at least 'Pre-intermediate (equivalent to IELTS 3.5)'.
For the IELTS Guarantee course, the required admission level may vary depending on the target score.

For the Business course, your level must be 'Intermediate'. Our Business course includes presentations and the class difficulty level is high, so the admission level is set higher than other schools.

If you do not know your level, you can measure your level through an interview. You can apply for the Admission Interview for free.
