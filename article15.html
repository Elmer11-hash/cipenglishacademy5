December 3, 2024

"Expansion of the Evening Program"

CIP ENGLISH is expanding its evening program starting December 1 to further enrich the study environment.

•The Spartan Course evening program will be extended from 2 to 3 hours, providing more time for focused learning and skill enhancement.
•Students in Semi-Spartan Courses will now have access to special classes and self-study sessions.

This expansion reflects our commitment to delivering a well-rounded and effective learning experience for all students.
